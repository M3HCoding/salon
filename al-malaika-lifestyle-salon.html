About Al-Malaika Life Style Salon

Welcome to Al-Malaika Life Style Salon, Hazaribagh's premier grooming destination for the whole family. Founded by Md Arif Hussain, our salon combines traditional techniques with modern styling to deliver exceptional results.

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing you with a relaxing experience and personalized service that enhances your natural beauty. We use only the highest quality products to ensure your hair and skin receive the best care possible.

Whether you're looking for a quick trim, a complete style transformation, or a special treatment for your little one, we have the expertise to meet all your grooming needs.
